The `/discord` command group manages the integration between your Discord account and MinionAH. Linking your accounts enables auction creation, viewing your listings, and other account-specific features.

## Why Link Your Account?

Linking your Discord account with MinionAH enables:

- Creating auction listings directly from Discord
- Viewing your active auctions with `/auctions myauctions`
- Managing your auction listings
- Receiving notifications about your auctions
- Seamless integration between Discord and the web platform

<Note>
Account linking is required for most auction management features. You can still browse auctions and check prices without linking.
</Note>

---

## /discord link

Link your Discord account to your MinionAH account.

### Parameters

This command takes no parameters.

### Usage

```bash
/discord link
```

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run the Command">
    Execute `/discord link` in Discord. The bot will check if you already have a linked account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Visit MinionAH.com">
    Click the "Link Discord Account" button in the bot's response. This will take you to your account settings on MinionAH.com.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete the Link">
    Log in to your MinionAH account (or create one) and authorize the Discord connection in your account settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Return to Discord">
    Once linked, return to Discord and you'll be able to use all account-specific features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Already Linked?

If your Discord account is already linked, you'll receive a message indicating this along with a link to your account settings where you can manage the connection.

### Example Response

<CodeGroup>
```text First Time Link
✅ Link your Discord account to MinionAH

To link your Discord account to MinionAH, click the button below. 
You will be redirected to your account page on MinionAH.com

[Link Discord Account]
```

```text Already Linked
⚠️ Discord Account Already Linked

Your Discord account is already linked to a MinionAH account. 
If you wish to link a different account, please unlink your 
current account first using the `/discord unlink` command or 
visit your account settings on MinionAH.com.

[Go to Account Settings]
```
</CodeGroup>

## /discord unlink

Unlink your Discord account from your MinionAH account.

<Warning>
Unlinking removes the connection between your Discord and MinionAH accounts. You will need to link again to use account-specific features.
</Warning>

### Parameters

This command takes no parameters.

### Usage

```bash
/discord unlink
```

### What Happens When You Unlink?

When you unlink your account:

- The connection between Discord and MinionAH is removed
- You can no longer create auctions from Discord
- You cannot view your auctions with `/auctions myauctions`
- You cannot delete auctions from Discord
- Your MinionAH account and data remain intact
- You can re-link at any time using `/discord link`

### Example Response

```text
✅ Discord account unlinked from MinionAH

Your Discord account has been successfully unlinked from your 
MinionAH account. If you wish to link it again in the future, 
you can use the `/discord link` command.
```

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Link button doesn't work">
    If the link button isn't working:
    
    1. Make sure you're clicking the button, not copying a link
    2. Try running the command again
    3. Visit https://minionah.com/profile/settings directly
    4. Ensure you're logged in to MinionAH
    5. Contact support if issues persist
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Commands still say I'm not linked">
    If commands indicate you're not linked after completing the process:
    
    1. Wait a few moments for the link to process
    2. Run `/discord link` to verify the connection status
    3. Check your account settings on MinionAH.com
    4. Try unlinking and re-linking your account
    5. Ensure you're using the same Discord account
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Want to link a different MinionAH account">
    To link a different MinionAH account:
    
    1. Run `/discord unlink` to remove the current connection
    2. Run `/discord link` to start the linking process
    3. Log in with the different MinionAH account
    4. Complete the authorization
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't find the link option on MinionAH.com">
    To find the Discord linking option:
    
    1. Log in to MinionAH.com
    2. Navigate to your profile settings
    3. Look for the "Connected Accounts" or "Integrations" section
    4. Find the Discord connection option
    5. Follow the on-screen instructions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="What Data is Shared?" icon="shield-check">
    Only your Discord username is shared with MinionAH to establish the account link. No sensitive data is transmitted.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Can I Revoke Access?" icon="lock">
    Yes, you can unlink your account at any time using `/discord unlink` or through your MinionAH account settings.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Is It Secure?" icon="key">
    The linking process uses secure OAuth authentication. Your Discord credentials are never shared with MinionAH.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="What Permissions Are Needed?" icon="user-check">
    The bot only needs permission to identify your Discord account. No additional permissions are required.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
